Our Partners

EU4Reconstruction is delivered through a network of 24 international, European, and Ukrainian partner organisations, each contributing unique expertise to programme implementation.

European Institutions

EU Partners

European Commission — DG NEAR

Belgium

The Directorate-General for European Neighbourhood Policy and Enlargement Negotiations provides strategic oversight, co-chairs the Steering Committee, and manages overall EU budget allocation.

EBRD — European Bank for Reconstruction and Development

United Kingdom

Provides project finance expertise, procurement standards, and technical due diligence for large infrastructure grants. Manages the programme's financial verification framework.

European Investment Bank

Luxembourg

Manages the blended finance facility, combining EU4R grants with concessional loans for larger-scale infrastructure and energy projects.

KfW Development Bank

Germany

Serves as the independent verification partner for a portfolio of Stream 1 and 2 projects. Provides technical engineering advisory services.

European Environment Agency

Denmark

Provides environmental compliance monitoring and advisory support, ensuring all funded projects meet EU environmental standards and climate-proofing requirements.

EU Delegation to Ukraine

Ukraine

The EU Delegation in Kyiv provides in-country liaison, political coordination, and communications support. Hosts the programme's main Kyiv office.

Ukrainian Partners

Government & Civil Society

🏛️

Ministry of Restoration of Ukraine

Ukraine 🇺🇦

Co-chairs the Steering Committee and provides national coordination across all oblasts. Responsible for the official registry of damaged infrastructure used for needs prioritisation.

🤝

Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Ukraine

Ukraine 🇺🇦

Facilitates international coordination and ensures alignment between EU4R activities and Ukraine's broader EU accession commitments under the Screening Process.

🌐

UNDP Ukraine

Ukraine 🇺🇦

Provides implementation support for Stream 4 capacity building activities. Manages the regional office network and coordinates civil society engagement across 10 oblasts.

🔨

Rebuilding Ukraine NGO

Ukraine 🇺🇦

Ukrainian structural engineering and project management NGO. Conducts building damage assessments and provides technical support to municipal applicants in 8 oblasts.

🏘️

Ukrainian Community Foundation

Ukraine 🇺🇦

Supports the Civil Society Advisory Panel and manages the CSO accreditation register. Ensures meaningful community participation in project design and monitoring.

⚡

Energy Community of Ukraine

Ukraine 🇺🇦

Provides technical advisory for Stream 3 projects, ensuring compliance with European Energy Community treaty obligations and REPowerEU standards.
