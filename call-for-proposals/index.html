Who Can Apply

Eligibility Criteria

EU4Reconstruction grants are open to a broad range of Ukrainian public and civil society actors. The following entity types are eligible:

  • ✓
    Ukrainian municipalities and city councils
    Any territorial community with legal personality and a mandate over the relevant infrastructure.
  • ✓
    Raion and oblast state administrations
    For cross-municipal or regional-scale infrastructure projects.
  • ✓
    Civil society organisations (CSOs)
    Ukrainian-registered NGOs and associations with demonstrated reconstruction experience. Must be accredited through the programme's CSO register.
  • ✓
    Public utilities and municipal enterprises
    For energy and water infrastructure projects, with a municipality as lead applicant.
–

Application Process

How to Apply

A clear, four-step process from concept to grant agreement.

1

Check Eligibility

Review the call guidelines and eligibility checklist. Attend an information session or contact the PMU with questions before applying.

2

Submit Concept Note

Submit a short concept note (max 8 pages) via the online application portal. Concept notes are assessed within 3 weeks.

3

Full Application

Shortlisted applicants are invited to submit a full application with technical design, budget, and implementation plan. Evaluation takes 6 weeks.

4

Grant Agreement

Successful applicants receive a formal grant offer and sign a grant agreement. First disbursement within 30 days of signing.
