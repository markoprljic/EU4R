Cookie Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how EU4Reconstruction uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on this website, and how you can control them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites work correctly, remember your preferences, and provide information to website owners about how the site is used.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They are usually set in response to actions you take, such as setting your cookie preferences. You can set your browser to block these cookies, but some parts of the site may not work as a result.

3. How to Manage Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time:

  • Cookie banner: When you first visit this site, a banner allows you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. Your choice is saved in your browser's local storage.
  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. See your browser's help documentation for instructions. Note that blocking all cookies may affect website functionality.
  • Opt-out tools: You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
