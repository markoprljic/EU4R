EU and Ukraine Sign €480M Additional Commitment for 2025

The European Commission has confirmed an additional €480 million allocation to the EU4Reconstruction programme, expanding coverage to three newly stabilised oblasts and accelerating delivery timelines across all four funding streams. The signing ceremony took place at the EU-Ukraine Summit in Brussels, attended by senior officials from both sides.
