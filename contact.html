Frequently Asked

Common Questions

Who is eligible to apply for EU4Reconstruction grants?

Ukrainian municipalities, raion and oblast administrations, civil society organisations registered in Ukraine, and public utility enterprises (with a municipality as lead applicant). See the eligibility page for full criteria.

Is co-funding required?

Yes, for most applicants under Streams 1–3. Co-funding rates range from 5%–15% depending on applicant type. Waivers are available for municipalities in active conflict zones. Stream 4 requires no co-funding.

How long does the evaluation process take?

Concept notes are assessed within 3 weeks of the deadline. Full applications take approximately 6 weeks. Fast-track procedures for emergency projects can complete assessment in 21 days.

Can we apply for more than one stream?

Yes, organisations can submit separate applications to different streams, provided each project is distinct and independently justifiable. Multi-stream projects are also possible where works are physically integrated.
